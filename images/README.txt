Adding Photos to La Sosta di Sismano Website
=============================================

Place your villa photos in this directory. The website references these filenames:

  hero.jpg        - Main hero image (wide landscape, 1600x900+ recommended)
  villa-1.jpg     - Gallery photo 1 (exterior or living area)
  villa-2.jpg     - Gallery photo 2 (bedroom or kitchen)
  villa-3.jpg     - Gallery photo 3 (view or garden)
  villa-4.jpg     - Gallery photo 4 (detail or surroundings)
  villa-5.jpg     - Gallery photo 5 (terrace or dining)
  villa-6.jpg     - Gallery photo 6 (landscape or nearby town)
  about-villa.jpg - Photo for the About page villa section
  umbria.jpg      - Photo for the About page Umbria section

Tips:
- JPG or WebP format work best for photos
- Aim for at least 1200px wide for best quality
- The site will still look good without photos (warm gradient placeholders)
- You can screenshot or download photos from your Airbnb listing
