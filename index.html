La Sosta di Sismano

A peaceful retreat in the green heart of Italy, with beautiful views across the Umbrian hills.

Nestled in the tiny medieval hamlet of Sismano, La Sosta di Sismano offers an authentic escape into the Umbrian countryside. Umbria — il cuore verde d'Italia, the green heart of Italy — is a land of rolling hills, ancient olive groves, and vineyards stretching to the horizon. Here, time moves a little slower and every evening brings a sunset you won't forget.

Why La Sosta

Everything you need for an unforgettable stay in the Italian countryside.

⛪

Location

Sismano sits just south of Todi, surrounded by medieval hill towns, with easy day trips to Orvieto, Assisi, Deruta, Spoleto, and even Rome.

🍴

Experience

Walk through olive groves, visit local wineries, browse Deruta's pottery workshops, or simply enjoy long Italian dinners under the stars.

🏡

Comfort

A thoughtfully decorated two-bedroom home for up to 4 guests, blending rustic Umbrian charm with everything you need to feel at home.
